Videos Generated by our Scene Exploration Model CamCtrl3D (supplements Figure 7 in paper)

We provide supplementary results for Figure 7 in the paper, showcasing our scene exploration model CamCtrl3D (Section 4.4 in the paper) on the RealEstate10K and DL3DV datasets, at different sampling speeds:

  • RealEstate10K, speed x4
  • DL3DV, speed x1
  • RealEstate10K, speed x2
  • RealEstate10K, speed x1
For RealEstate10K, we show videos at a single sampling speed (either x1, x2, or x4), however the assets directory includes all three speeds for each video. In each video, we show the initial image (left), next to the camera trajectory (middle), and the generated video (right). For comparison with ground-truth videos that have the same initial image and camera trajectory, please see section Comparison Between Videos Generated by CamCtrl3D and Ground Truth.
